Meet Your Doctor

He is the youngest pulmonologist from Gujarat to have completed DNB in his first try. His skills, expertise and experience are truly remarkable

Dr Jaykumar Mehta

Dr. Jaykumar Mehta
M.B.B.S. (Gold Medalist)                               
M.D. (Respiratory Medicine)                                                          
D. N. B. (Respiratory Diseases)                                                         
PD Fellow (Pulmonology) (CMC,Vellore)

Dr. Jaykumar Mehta is amongst the first pulmonologist from Gujarat to undergo post M.D. modular training – a fellowship program 
in the specialty of Pulomonology from the prestigious Christian Medical college, Vellore. He has specialized training in 
bronchoscopy and medical thoracoscopy with competence in diagnosing and managing drug resistant tuberculosis, 
other respiratory infections, pre operative pulmonary evaluation & peri-operative pulmonary complications, OSAS 
and other sleep disordered breathing, IPF, sarcoidosis, connective tissue diseases related ILDs and other DPLDs, 
Pulmonary embolism and vasculitis, obstructive airway diseases, pleural diseases, malignancies involving lungs & pleurae.

At present, He is providing his services as full time consultant & interventional pulmonologist at Zydus Hospital, Ahmedabad.

Research:
1. PG Thesis: A study of diagnostic approach to Interstitial Lung Diseases.
2. Fellow Thesis: Role of Bronchoscopy in evaluation of Diffuse Parenchymal Lung Diseases.
3. Current Study: Retrospective analysis of the yield of flexible bronchoscopy and guided procedures at tertiary care center in Gujarat

Honors:
1. “Swami Shri Krishnanandji (Bhadran) Gold Plated Medal” From Sardar Patel University, Vallabh Vidyanagar for performance in Third M.B.B.S. (Part II) Examinations.
2. “ Best Emerging Consultant” Award (2017) from Honorable Shri Pankaj Patel sir, Chairman, Zydus Hospitals.
